# Browse AP Subjects

Free AP Practice supports 20+ AP subjects for unlimited question generation.

## Pages

- `/subjects` — subject picker
- `/summer` — summer Unit 1 preview planning
- `/practice/{subject-slug}` — SEO practice landing pages

## Discovery
